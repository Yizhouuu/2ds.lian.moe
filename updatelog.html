Reforging Overhaul 重铸改革

This update changes completely the reforging mechanics! It is now less random and more progression oriented, it also removes the unique tier and adds another called Mythic, which has similar stats. Along with this, staves and wands are now also reforgable!
这个更新完全改变了重铸机制！现在它更少随机，更加注重进展，还移除了独特的等级，并添加了另一个叫做神话的等级，它具有类似的属性。除此之外，法杖和魔杖现在也可以重铸！

There are now 3 tiers of reforging items (that replace the amethyst required before), each allowing you to reforge to certain qualities.
现在有 3 个重铸物品的等级（取代了之前所需的紫水晶），每个等级都允许你重铸到特定的品质。

Limestone, found in the overworld, allows you to reforge to Common, Uncommon, Rare and Epic. While Pyrite, found in the Nether, allows to reforge to Uncommon, Rare, Epic and Legendary. Galena, found in the End, allows to Rare, Epic, Legendary and Mythic.
石灰岩，在主世界中找到，允许你重铸到普通、罕见、稀有和史诗。而黄铁矿，在地狱中找到，允许重铸到罕见、稀有、史诗和传奇。方铅矿，在末地中找到，允许重铸到稀有、史诗、传奇和神话。

Most of the qualities of your items will be ported over, but Unique quality items won't be converted into Mythic.
您的物品大部分品质将被转移，但独特品质的物品不会转换为神话品质。

Fixes & Brews! 修复和酿造！

This update polishes out some issues from the last update (mostly related to EMI), while also bringing small additions like more Copycats, rotating chipped blocks and more!
此更新解决了上次更新中的一些问题（主要与 EMI 相关），同时还增加了一些小功能，如更多的 Copycats，旋转的芯片块等等！

The modpack now comes with VanillAA enabled by default! This shaderpack integrates TAA into Minecraft, making the game look better on distances and fixing the "mipmap issues" some mods have. Performance impact may depend on your system but most systems won't have any noticeable impact as after all it's only adding TAA. If you have a really old system and are getting low fps, you might want to disable this shader.
现在默认启用 VanillAA 模组包！这个着色器包将 TAA 集成到 Minecraft 中，使游戏在远处看起来更好，并修复了一些模组的"mipmap 问题"。性能影响可能取决于您的系统，但大多数系统不会有任何明显的影响，因为它只是添加了 TAA。如果您的系统非常旧且帧率较低，您可能希望禁用此着色器。
